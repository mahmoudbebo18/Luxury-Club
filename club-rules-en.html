Club Rules

Name, address, owner, and basic law

  • The name of the club is “the Luxury Club” and is located at the Al Jawhara office center on Prince Mohamed bin Abdulaziz Street in Riyadh's Al Olaya district (the “Club”).
  • The club is owned and managed by Al Musaysiah Business Services (the “owner”).
  • The owner provides the club with the necessary sites and facilities to carry out all club activities in accordance with the general terms and these rules. The owner will be solely responsible for all club operating expenses, staff appointment and dismissal, payment of salaries, food supply and all matters Others that include spending money or bearing costs. The owner also appoints the club manager (called “manager”) who has the day-to-day management responsibilities of the club.
  • These rules are called “rules” and govern the operation of the club. It is a requirement for club membership that members agree to be bound by these rules (and their amendments from time to time).

Objectives  

  • The club was created to promote and promote non-political, social and recreational activities, and to provide a high-quality club that offers food and beverages in accordance with the laws and regulations of the club (as amended from time to time).

General Committee  

  • The administration of the club’s business is at the disposal of the General Committee (called the “General Committee”), which exercises the powers conferred on it by these rules and other powers of management to which it is occasionally authorized by the owner.

Members of the General Committee  

  • The composition of the General Committee shall be as follows:
    • All directors and directors of the Company shall be ex officio members during their tenure.
    • The club manager who is an ex officio member during his term of office.

Meetings of the General Committee  

  • The General Committee shall meet as and when necessary. A meeting of the General Committee may be requested at any time by any member of the Bureau upon notification of the member of the Committee in the building.
  • A quorum shall constitute any two members of the Committee.

Membership  

  • If necessary, the club includes members or offers temporary membership from time to time, for commercial reasons.
  • Women and men of a respected social status who are at least 18 years of age can have membership.

Proposals for membership and due attention   

  • Each member and candidate for membership must provide the owner with a copy of the ID card, proof of residential address and commercial address (which must be a physical address, not a post office address or a “sponsorship” address) when the owner requests it.
  • Each member and candidate for membership must provide the owner with the necessary information upon request.
  • No person shall be permitted to enjoy the privileges of membership if the General Committee rejects the application for membership, at its sole discretion.
  • Members referendum After closing the preferred membership door, you choose the club's location and a group of their interests. A week, the best environment for high-end entertainment is suitable for them.

Membership fees  

  • Membership fees, where appropriate, shall be payable and shall be paid upon the selection of the member and shall be paid annually.
  • Membership fees are announced in advance each year, and are based in part on past benefits and services used by the member.

Members address and notifications  

  • Each member must notify the owner in writing or update their data on the private application when changes are made to his or her residential address or business address and provide proof of this new address. Members must provide their actual residential and commercial addresses (not just an office Mail or “Care” address).
  • Any notice required under these Rules may be sent by pre-paid letter or email addressed to the member at the last physical address provided to the owner. If sent on an address in the Saudi Arabian Kingdom, he will be handed over two working days after the dispatch If sent on an address outside the Saudi Arabian Kingdom, it will be delivered seven working days after the dispatch.

Rights and duties of members  

  • Each member of the Club shall be entitled, subject to these Rules, to use and enjoy the Club building and the facilities provided by the Owner (in association with other members of the Club), but solely because of their membership, they shall not be liable for the daily costs of the Owner Alternatively, some club areas may be reserved for certain members. The owner may not allow entry to anyone who acts improperly or anyone who sees him or her as abnormal, or may be required to leave the club.

Members and their guests  

  • It is prohibited to bring food, drinks, articles and items to the Club building, and may be prohibited from entering if the member attempts to enter the building with alcoholic drinks or if he has illegal substances.
  • It's better to wear a proper uniform.
  • No member or guest may participate in the club's activities on behalf of anyone who is not in the building at that time, and no member may participate or attempt to participate unless he or she is present at the club at that time.
  • For the convenience of all members and their guests, public photography with mobile phones or other mobile devices is not permitted to infringe on the privacy of other members of the club.
  • Cameras, other recording devices or transmitters may not be used in the Club without express permission.
  • The owner will not discriminate or allow others to discriminate against employees or members and their guests intentionally on grounds of sex, age, race or disability. The owner may suspend or terminate the membership if the member discriminates against employees, another member or guest of any of these Exponents.
  • The owner will not tolerate subversive behavior. Conduct is “subversive” in the event that the property of the owner is deliberately damaged or threatened, or the use of offensive, improper, or insulting words or actions against the members, their guests, and any employee, or if the member is under the influence of the prohibitions.

Guests  

  • Members can bring guests (at least 18 years old) and host them in the club. The guest is required to provide the club with personal information, residential address and business address, as required by the owner.
  • The host member is responsible for the host's compliance with strict rules. Members are responsible for their guests, so members must remain with their guests at all times, and when they leave the member, their guests must also leave.
  • Members may not bring more than two guests on any occasion except with the prior approval of a member of the General Committee, in accordance with the capacity of the Club.
  • No person whose membership has been revoked, suspended under the Club Rules or whose application for membership in the Club has been denied or canceled under the General Rules may be brought as a guest except with the prior approval of a member of the General Committee.
  • A guest may be denied access to the club or be required to leave the club at any time at the discretion of a member of the General Committee without giving any reason.
  • Any guest who is unable or unwilling to provide a copy of the identification and proof of their residential or commercial address upon entry to the Club may be permitted access at the discretion of the owner's management on a single occasion but must provide an appropriate identity if re-entered. Although there are no documents An appropriate identity, the guest will be required to provide, verbally, identification and other details that may be required from time to time by the owner, and the owner will obtain the guest's adequate information to hold for identification purposes. A guest who has not submitted documents is prohibited Demonstrate an appropriate identity and/or other details, unless such documents/details are provided and until the owner agrees in his sole discretion to allow the guest to enjoy social and recreational activities at the club.

Confidentiality, privacy, data collection and processing

  • Members must respect the confidentiality of other members' affairs, which takes place at the club's premises, and must not be disclosed under any circumstances to persons who are not affiliated with the club.
  • As part of the application process, a copy of the Club Privacy Policy is provided to all candidates for membership and guests. It is also published and made available within the Club and on the Club website [www.the-luxury-club.com] and sent via email to all members And guests. The owner will obtain and use personal data about members or their guests in accordance with the Club's Privacy and Data Protection Policy legislation.
  • Members are not allowed to use any recording device that contains images, sound, or both in the building. Members may not use any device that transmits live photos from within the club or photos of its employees or customers outside the building.
  • As set forth in the Privacy Policy, the owner is required by law to process and retain certain information provided by members, candidates for membership, guests and/or the owner in accordance with the Privacy Policy, whether approved On request or not, and/or still members of the club.

Security

  • The owner may use security cameras with voice recording and/or other security systems, both inside and outside the building for the purpose of ensuring the safety and security of the club, its members and its guests.

Internal Regulations

  • The General Committee may from time to time prepare, change and repeal regulations (which do not conflict with these rules) to regulate the internal affairs of the Club and the behavior of members and their guests. Regulations are complementary to club rules.

Cancel and suspend membership

  • The General Committee may, at its sole discretion, at any time without notice and for any reason, revoke the membership of any member of the Club without giving any explanation.
  • Any member whose membership is revoked by the Rules is immediately excluded from the Club, all membership privileges including uncrystallized loyalty or other rewards are lost and any amounts due to the Owner are immediately paid.
  • The General Committee may, at its sole discretion, at any time, without notice and for any reason, suspend the membership of any member of the Club without giving any explanation.
  • Any person whose membership is suspended under this rule is immediately excluded from the Club, during which all membership privileges are lost and any amounts due to the Owner are immediately paid.

Resignation of members

  • A member's resignation must be notified in writing and made available when received by the owner.

Club ownership and property of members and guests

  • No member or guest may remove any property, newspaper or anything else belonging to the owner from the Club building without permission from the Club Administrator.
  • The club, members of the General Committee and the owner shall not be liable for any loss or damage to the property of members or guests in the building, whether or not the owner or his or her employees have been assigned to care for them.

Mail

  • No member of the Club may give the Club Address as his or her address for any purpose.

Use of Club Name

  • No member of the Club may use the Club name or membership in any form of advertising at all, whether it is a reference to the Club or membership in the Notebook, Business Cards, Commercial Buildings or in any other way.

Animals

  • No pets are allowed in the club.

Opening hours

  • The club starts and is ready to receive members every day during the owner's set hours of time

Complaints and disputes

  • All complaints shall be received in writing to the Director or Director of the Club who shall transmit them to the General Committee. Any complaints are investigated and dealt with by the General Committee as it deems appropriate. No member or guest may be reprimanded by an employee of the owner. go ahead.
  • Any complaints or disputes about conduct or action within the club shall be dealt with in accordance with the dispute resolution procedures written by the owner. Members are provided with the opportunity to refer any unresolved complaint or dispute to the courts and subject to these rules and any dispute arising out of or in connection with the rules or otherwise in Context of member's membership in Saudi law.

Modifications

  • The owner may change, cancel or complete the rules at any time. These rules, as amended from time to time, must be kept posted on a bulletin board or displayed prominently or on an electronic device in the club for members to be informed, and all members must be fully informed With it.
